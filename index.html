Step 1 of 6

Connect your API key

Dino Diagnostic runs entirely in your browser. Your Anthropic API key is stored locally and used only to generate your diagnostic — it never touches our servers.

ℹ
Don't have a key? Get one at console.anthropic.com. New accounts include free credits.
Your key is saved to localStorage on this device only.

How your key is used

✓ One small verification call when you connect (~100 tokens)
✓ One indexing call when your materials are processed
✓ Question batches during the test (5 at a time)
✓ Two calls to generate your final report and gap test
